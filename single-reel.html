After a successful collaboration with Grammy winner Boi-1da on Marvel/Playstation's Miles Morales score, John Paesano teams up with Egypt's biggest hip hop producer Molotof for the score to Marvel’s new series, Moon Knight. The show’s theme features Molotof’s beats woven under traditional Egyptian percussion and Paesano’s thematic orchestral score.

Paesano, known for projects such as Marvel's Daredevil, Marvel Defenders, 20th Century Fox "The Maze Runner Trilogy", and Marvel/PlayStation Spider-Man is a composer of Lebanese descent, he has long looked for a project that would combine his modern film scoring with his middle eastern heritage.
